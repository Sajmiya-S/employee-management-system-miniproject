         PLEASE READ THE INSTRUCTIONS CAREFULLY BEFORE STARTING REGISTRATION 
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 USERNAME:
   - Must contain only letters or numbers (no special characters or spaces).
   - Should be more than 3 characters long.
   - Cannot be entirely numeric.

 PASSWORD:
   - Minimum 6 characters long.
   - Must include at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one digit, 
     and one special character (@ # $ % ^ & * - _ !).
   - You will need to confirm your password for verification.

 ROLE ID:
   - Enter 1 for Manager or 0 for Employee.

 MANAGER REGISTRATION:
   - You will need to provide: Full Name, Department, Contact Number, and Email ID.
   - Department name must match an existing department (e.g., HR, FINANCE, IT, SALES, etc.).
   - Contact number must contain exactly 10 digits.
   - Email ID must be valid (example: name@example.com).

 EMPLOYEE REGISTRATION:
   - Requires a valid Manager ID for verification.
   - You will need to provide: Full Name, Department, Job Title, Date of Joining, 
     Salary, Contact Number, and Email ID.
   - Date format should be DD-MM-YYYY.
   - Department name must match an existing department.
   - Contact number must contain exactly 10 digits.
   - Email ID must be valid (example: name@example.com).

 Any invalid input will ask you to re-enter the field.
